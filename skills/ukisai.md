# We build AI with purpose.

## About

Serbia based applied AI lab founded in 2023. We develop public-facing AI products, run applied research, and work closely with companies to design, prototype, and deploy AI solutions. Our approach combines expertise with practical execution, moving ideas from research to real-world use.

## Our Mission

To bridge AI research and real-world impact by building practical AI products, advancing research, and partnering with companies to solve meaningful problems.

# UkisAI has been selected for the AWS Startups Program!

![UkisAI has been selected for the AWS Startups Program!](https://ukisai.com/news/featured.png)

Validation is a powerful fuel for any startup, and getting tapped by Amazon Web Services is about as high-octane as it gets. We are thrilled to announce that UkisAI has been selected for the prestigious AWS Startups Program. We're building in public, and now, we have the cloud giant backing our every move.

# Important Note: TrueStay is adding rate limits and a premium tier

From day one, TrueStay has been free and open to everyone. That's not changing. But building a product that people around the world rely on every day comes with real costs - designers, engineers, servers, and the external services that power our AI. To keep TrueStay running and growing, we're introducing rate limits and a premium tier.

Free users will still have full access to TrueStay's core experience - analyze reviews, get honest summaries, and make better travel decisions. Premium unlocks extra power: higher usage limits, faster processing, and additional features designed for frequent travelers who want the most out of TrueStay.

This isn't about locking things behind a paywall. It's about building something sustainable so we can keep improving TrueStay for everyone. We're a small team with big ambitions, and this step lets us keep doing what we love - making travel decisions easier for millions of people.

We'll be rolling out this update in the following days. Stay tuned.

# Introducing UkisAI Academy - free AI education for everyone

AI is changing the way we work and live, and it's only going to accelerate. We've seen it firsthand - building products, working with companies, and talking to people who want to get into AI but don't know where to start. That's the gap we want to close.

UkisAI Academy is a education program for anyone who wants to learn how to actually use AI in their work and build their own AI-powered products and apps. It doesn't matter if you're a student, a professional switching careers, or someone who's just curious - if you want to learn, Academy is for you.

## How it works

Academy offers online courses built in collaboration with other people and companies who are already doing great work in AI. Every course is hands-on - you'll build real things, not just watch lectures. And when you finish, you get a certificate to show for it.

## Why we're doing this

We're entering a new era where AI is the main player in how we work, create, and solve problems. Our goal is simple - teach as many people as possible how to utilize AI so they're ready for what's coming, not playing catch-up.

Our first program - the FON AI Bootcamp - is already running in partnership with the Faculty of Organisational Sciences, University of Belgrade, powered by AWS for Startups and Cursor. More courses and collaborations are on the way.

# UkisAI x FON - AI Bootcamp: Build your own AI product

Our first Academy course is here - the UkisAI x FON AI Bootcamp. We partnered with the [Faculty of Organisational Sciences at the University of Belgrade](https://fon.bg.ac.rs/), with support from [AWS for Startups](https://aws.amazon.com/startups?lang=en-US) and [Cursor](https://www.cursorserbia.com/), to create a free 5-week program where you go from zero to building and deploying your own AI product.

This isn't a lecture series. Every week you'll be writing code, building something, and walking away with a working project. By the end of the five weeks, you'll have a fully functional AI app deployed to a live URL - something you actually built yourself.

The bootcamp is free and open to everyone. You don't need any prior AI experience - just a laptop and the motivation to learn. Whether you're a first-year student or already working in tech, this program is for you.

The bootcamp kicks off on March 28th, every Saturday from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Seats are limited and those who attend will receive a certificate, but we'll keep all recordings online so anyone can visit and watch them for free.

[Register here](https://luma.com/6dipoq8o)
